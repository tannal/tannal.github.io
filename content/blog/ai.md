# What is intelligence

The intelligence is the ability to do something but don't know how.

## People has intelligence

- You can speak a language without knowing how to control your mouth or tongue

- You can ride a bike without knowing how to keep balance.

- You can feel what is good or bad, what is fair or unfair, but you can not explain what is fair or good.

- Despite you have no idea how to speak a language, but you can teach your son or girl to speak.


# What is Artificial Intelligence

We already show that

1. people has intelligence that is can do something but don't know how.

2. despite no idea how to do it, people can teach other people to learn something that they don't even know how to do it themselves.

The second point is crucial to define Artificial Intelligence.The only thing we need to do is change "teach other people" to "teach machine".

Or even further teach machine to do something they have no idea how, plus after that the machine can teach the ability to other machine or people.

So Artificial Intelligence is something that can do things like people, and can teach other people/machine to do things, but have no idea how itself.
